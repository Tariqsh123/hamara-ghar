Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and the property buying process

Are all properties verified before listing?
Yes, we have a rigorous verification process. Every property listed on PakEstate undergoes thorough legal clearance checks, ownership document verification, and physical inspection by our team before being listed. We ensure all properties have clear titles and are free from any legal disputes.
Do you deal only in approved housing societies?
We primarily focus on legally approved housing societies to ensure our clients' investments are secure and have proper development plans. We verify the approval status of all projects with relevant authorities before listing any property. This protects our clients from investing in unauthorized or illegal housing schemes.
How can overseas Pakistanis invest through you?
We specialize in assisting overseas Pakistanis with property investment in Pakistan. Our dedicated overseas desk handles all documentation, legal processes, and property management remotely. We offer video property tours, digital documentation, and secure payment gateways for international transactions. Over 40% of our clients are overseas Pakistanis.
Is the initial consultation really free?
Yes, our initial consultation is completely free with no obligations. Our experts will analyze your requirements, budget, and investment goals to provide personalized recommendations. We'll help you understand the market, legal requirements, financing options, and potential returns without any charges.
Do you offer properties on installment plans?
Yes, we have partnerships with several approved developers and housing societies that offer flexible installment plans. We can help you find properties with payment plans ranging from 6 months to 5 years, with down payments as low as 10-20%. We also assist with home financing through our banking partners.
What legal support do you provide?
We provide comprehensive legal support including title verification, document preparation, registration assistance, and NOC processing. Our in-house legal team works with you throughout the transaction to ensure all legal requirements are met and your investment is fully protected.
