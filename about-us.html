About Us

Welcome to Hamara Ghar, your trusted destination for finding your dream home in Pakistan. At Hamara Ghar, we are passionate about making the process of buying, selling, or renting property simple, transparent, and hassle-free. Our platform connects homeowners, buyers, and renters with reliable listings, up-to-date market insights, and expert guidance, ensuring that every decision you make is informed and confident. Whether you are searching for your first home, upgrading to a new property, or investing in real estate, Hamara Ghar is here to make your journey smooth and rewarding.

How Did It All Start?

Hamara Ghar began as the vision of two passionate entrepreneurs who wanted to make real estate accessible and hassle-free for everyone in Pakistan. Starting as a small venture, they worked tirelessly to connect buyers, sellers, and renters through a reliable platform.

Our first funding round in 2023 attracted several angel investors, followed by two more major investments in 2024 and 2025, helping us grow into a leading property platform nationwide.
